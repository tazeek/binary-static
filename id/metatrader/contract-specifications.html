Bagaimana cara membaca tabel di atas

The Forex is typically traded in lots. One standard lots is equivalent to 100,000 units. Each time you open a position on a currency symbol, you can start with a minimum transaction of 0.01 lots.

Leverage indicates how much capital you can control based on your initial deposit. For example, a 100:1 leverage will allow you to control up to USD 100,000 using only USD 1,000 of your own money as deposit.

To learn more, read our Margin Policy that further explains our 100% forced liquidation level for Forex.

Catatan penting pada tingkat swap (pendanaan semalam)

If you keep any positions open overnight, an interest adjustment will be made to your trading account as indication of the cost required to keep your position open. This adjustment is based on interbank lending rates, on top of a 2% fee.

The interest adjustment is calculated in points, meaning we will convert the relevant interbank lending rates to points in the base currency.

The swap rate will depend on the direction of the position you take:

  • Posisi Long: Kami mengenakan 2% biaya di atas suku bunga kredit antar bank. Bagaimana kita menghitung biaya bunga untuk posisi long?
  • Posisi Short: Kami mengenakan 2% biaya di atas suku bunga kredit antar bank. Bagaimana kita menghitung biaya bunga untuk posisi short?

Please take note that our swap rate also depends on the time and days you hold your positions open:

  • You will be subjected to swap rates if you keep a position open past 23:59:59 GMT.
  • Positions that are still open on Wednesday at 23:59:59 GMT will be charged three times the swap rate to account for weekends, a standard practice for all Forex brokers.
  • Our swap rate may also be adjusted to take holidays into account.

Bagaimana cara membaca tabel di atas

CFD (Contract for Difference): CFD merupakan instrumen yang memungkinkan Anda untuk mendapatkan keuntungan dengan berspekulasi pada pergerakan naik atau turun dari instrumen. Laba/rugi diperkirakan sebagai fungsi dari perbedaan dalam membeli/menjual harga dari instrumen yang mendasari.

Sebagai contoh, CFD pada Indeks Volatilitas menawarkan kontrak 1 USD per poin. Jika Anda membeli kontrak dengan Volume 1.0 untuk Indeks Volatilitas 100 dengan harga ask terkini adalah USD 20,000 dan menjualnya pada harga bid USD 20,010, maka keuntungan bersih Anda adalah dihitung sebagai berikut:

(Harga Jual - Harga Beli) x Volume x Harga Per Poin = Profit

Yaitu: (20010 - 20000) x 1 x 1 = USD 10

Setiap kali Anda membuka posisi pada simbol Indeks Volatilitas, Anda bisa mulai dengan volume transaksi minimal 0,01 (ukuran trading), yang diterjemahkan menjadi USD 0,01 per pergerakan titik indeks.

Margin indicates how much investment you can control based on your initial capital. For example, a 0.2% margin will allow you to control up to USD 500,000 using only USD 1,000 of your own money as deposit.

To learn more, read our Margin Policy that further explains our 50% forced liquidation level for Volatility Indices.
