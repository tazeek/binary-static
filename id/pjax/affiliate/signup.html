A client is someone who has been referred through one of your marketing tools and who has deposited money into their Binary.com account. They must fulfill the following criteria:

  • Have not previously been a Binary.com customer.
  • Have made the minimum deposit.
  • Are over 18.

Siapa saja yang berumur dari 18 tahun keatas [dan sudah membaca dan menyetujui syarat dan ketentuan Binary.com] dan tidak bertempat tinggal di negara 'tertentu' dapat bertrading di platform Binary.com.
